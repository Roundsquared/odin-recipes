The Perfect Fried Egg

Description

This is the one basic component for a tasty breakfast, made at home!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Melt butter in pan over medium heat, wait for bubbles.
  2. Once bubbles form, crack your egg with one hand like a badass, and drop it in.
  3. Take egg out once your egg white looks white and not that weird clear color.
  4. Flip dat hoe
